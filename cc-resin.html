CC Resin

Clear Cast Resin

Product Overview

CC Resin (Clear Cast Resin) is a high-quality unsaturated polyester resin formulated for clear casting, embedding, and decorative applications. It cures to a transparent, glass-like finish with excellent hardness and durability, making it ideal for artwork, jewelry, handicrafts, table tops, and encapsulation of decorative objects.

Its low shrinkage, high clarity, and smooth surface finish make Clear Cast Resin a preferred choice for industries that require aesthetic appeal along with strength and durability.

Key Features

  • Crystal clear, transparent finish
  • High gloss and smooth surface
  • Low shrinkage for bubble-free casting
  • Excellent adhesion to fillers, pigments, and decorative materials
  • Good mechanical strength and scratch resistance
  • Easy to pigment or dye for colored castings
  • Compatible with hand pouring and mold casting
  • Cost-effective compared to epoxy casting resins

Advantages of CC Resin

Crystal-clear finish for decorative and artistic applications

Low shrinkage ensures bubble-free, stable casting

Enhances appearance with high gloss and transparency

Strong, durable, and scratch-resistant

Suitable for art, furniture, decorative, and encapsulation projects

Applications of CC Resin (Clear Cast)

  1. Art & Decorative Industry:
    • Resin jewelry, ornaments, and keychains
    • Decorative crafts, sculptures, and models
    • Resin art panels and wall décor
  2. Furniture & Interiors:
    • Table tops and counter tops with decorative embeds
    • Transparent panels and coatings
    • Decorative laminates and surface finishes
  3. Industrial & Utility Applications:
    • Encapsulation of electronic parts and decorative items
    • Prototyping and model making
    • Transparent castings requiring durability
  4. Embedding & Encapsulation:
    • Preservation of flowers, coins, and collectibles
    • Casting of decorative stones, shells, and artifacts
    • Clear coatings for display products

Storage & Handling

  • Store in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated place.
  • Protect from direct sunlight and heat sources.
  • Ideal storage temperature: 20°C – 25°C.
  • Shelf life: 3 months (if stored properly in sealed containers).
  • Use with protective gloves, goggles, and mask.
