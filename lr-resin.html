Lamination Resin

Product Overview

Lamination Resin is a specially formulated unsaturated polyester resin used for lamination, coating, and surface finishing applications. It is designed to provide excellent adhesion, transparency, and durability, making it ideal for protective coatings, fiberglass laminates, decorative finishes, and industrial products.

The resin cures to a clear, glossy, and hard finish, enhancing both the appearance and strength of the laminated product. Lamination Resin is widely used in FRP products, furniture, decorative panels, and surface coatings.

Key Features

  • High clarity and transparency after curing
  • Strong adhesion to substrates (wood, FRP, paper, fabric, etc.)
  • Smooth and glossy surface finish
  • Good mechanical strength and scratch resistance
  • Resistant to moisture, mild chemicals, and environmental factors
  • Easy to process with hand lay-up and brush/roller applications
  • Compatible with pigments, fillers, and fiberglass reinforcements

Advantages of Lamination Resin

Provides a smooth, glossy, and transparent finish

Enhances strength and durability of laminated surfaces

Excellent adhesion to wood, FRP, and decorative substrates

Protects against moisture, scratches, and chemicals

Ideal for both industrial and decorative applications

Applications of Lamination Resin

  1. Furniture & Decorative Industry:
    • Lamination of plywood, MDF boards, and particle boards
    • Decorative sheets, panels, and laminates
    • Table tops and wooden furniture finishes
  2. Construction & Interior:
    • Wall panels, ceiling sheets, and partitions
    • Marble chips and stone surface lamination
    • Flooring laminates and protective coatings
  3. FRP (Fiber Reinforced Plastic) Industry:
    • Laminating fiberglass components
    • Transparent coatings for FRP sheets and products
    • Water tanks, roofing sheets, and protective linings
  4. Art & Decorative Applications:
    • Casting and embedding decorative objects
    • Resin-based art, handicrafts, and ornaments
    • Glossy top coats for displays and signage

Storage & Handling

  • Store in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated area away from direct sunlight.
  • Recommended storage temperature: 20°C – 25°C.
  • Keep containers tightly closed when not in use.
  • Shelf life: 3 months (under proper storage conditions).
  • Always handle with protective gloves, goggles, and mask.
