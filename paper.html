Paper

Product Grades

LR-952

BILLIONS™ LR-952 pigment is an alumina and phosphate treated rutile titanium dioxide pigment made by the sulphate process. Specially designed for decorative paper laminates used in wall panels and furniture. Also used in rigid PVC and outdoor plastics.

Data Sheet Sample

BLR-852

BILLIONS™ BLR-852 pigment is an alumina and phosphate treated rutile titanium dioxide pigment produced by the chloride process. Designed for decorative paper laminates, it offers excellent opacity, brightness, high light fastness, and retention properties.

Data Sheet Sample

VVTi-Opati

VVTi OpaTi is a high-purity anatase TiO₂ with excellent tinting strength, neutral white tone, and suitability for applications like paints, plastics, rubber, paper, and ceramics.
