General Purpose Titanium Dioxide

Product Grades

R6618

R6618(T) is a rutile titanium dioxide pigment produced by zirconium, aluminum inorganic and organic surface treated, designed to give average particle size distribution, good whiteness, high gloss, good dispersion, good hiding power and high weather resistance.

R996

BILLIONS™ R-996 pigment is a zirconia and alumina treated rutile titanium dioxide pigment produced by the sulfate process. It is highly durable and versatile, delivering good brightness, opacity, and easy dispersibility.

R2195

The product is rutile titanium dioxide pigment with silicon, aluminum inorganic surface treatment, organic surface treatment, with high whiteness, good dispersibility, tinting strength, and high weathering resistance.

BLR-895

BILLIONS™ BLR-895 pigment is a coatings grade titanium dioxide pigment manufactured by the chloride process. It has outstanding dispersion performance, opacity, gloss, and weatherability, making it suitable for both interior and exterior coatings.

Data Sheet Sample

CR-718

CR718 is a zirconium, aluminum and organic surface treated rutile titanium dioxide pigment manufactured by chloride process. It has excellent opacity, gloss, dispersibility, and high weatherability, making it suitable for many applications.

RC-822
