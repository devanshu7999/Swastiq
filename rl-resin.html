RL Resin

Roofing & Lining Resin

Product Overview

RL Resin is a specialized unsaturated polyester resin engineered for demanding roofing and lining applications. It is formulated to provide exceptional weather resistance, UV stability, and mechanical durability, making it the ideal choice for creating long-lasting, protective surfaces for both new construction and maintenance projects.

This resin forms a seamless, monolithic membrane that is highly resistant to water ingress, chemical attack, and environmental degradation. Its superior adhesion to various substrates ensures a reliable, long-term solution for protecting valuable assets from corrosion and leaks.

Key Features

  • Excellent UV and weather resistance for outdoor durability
  • High resistance to water, chemicals, and corrosion
  • Superior mechanical strength and flexibility
  • Forms a seamless, waterproof membrane
  • Strong adhesion to concrete, metal, and FRP substrates
  • Easy to apply with standard roofing and lining techniques

Advantages of RL Resin

Provides long-term protection against leaks and corrosion

Increases the service life of roofs, tanks, and floors

Cost-effective alternative to complete replacement

Maintains performance in harsh weather conditions

Versatile for both new projects and repairs

Applications of RL Resin

  1. Industrial & Commercial Roofing:
    • Manufacturing of FRP corrugated and plain roofing sheets
    • Seamless waterproofing for flat roofs
    • Protective coatings for metal roofs
  2. Linings & Containment:
    • Chemical storage tank linings
    • Waterproofing for water tanks and reservoirs
    • Corrosion-resistant lining for pipes and ducts
  3. Construction & Infrastructure:
    • Protective coatings for concrete surfaces
    • Waterproofing for basements and foundations
    • Durable, chemical-resistant industrial flooring

Storage & Handling

  • Store in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated area, away from direct sunlight and heat.
  • Ideal storage temperature: 20°C – 25°C.
  • Keep containers sealed to prevent contamination and moisture ingress.
  • Shelf life: 3 months (when stored in original sealed containers).
  • Handle with appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE).
